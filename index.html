Hi! I'm Cleo.

developmental psychology researcher and educator by day,
language-learning enthusiast, R Shiny coder, photographer, and artist by night.

Research

I'm interested in the development of theory of mind (i.e., the understanding that others have thoughts, desires, beliefs, intentions, and emotions that may differ from one's own) and how it relates to deception, trust, executive function, and language.

Selected Projects

Parental mental state talk in two contexts:
Parents’ cognitive sentential complements are positively associated with children’s theory of mind (Tay & Ding, 2022)

Detecting lies through others’ eyes:
Children use perceptual access cues to evaluate listeners’ beliefs about informants’ deception (Tay et al., 2024)
